Welcome Message

Fostering Discipline, Unity, & Character in Our Youth

Established as a key regional center of the Bharat Scouts & Guides, Topdara Division Ajmer operates in accordance with national directives to design and run training programs for school-level scouts, girl guides, and community volunteers.

Our directives are geared towards promoting physical, mental, and spiritual resilience. Through active drills, leadership certification, and environmental action projects, we connect education with selfless national service.

What We Do

OUR CORE OBJECTIVES

Empowering volunteers through structured modules to serve the community and build character.

CAMPING & NATURE

Pitching canvas tents, fire management, wilderness exploration, and safety drills teach scouts resilience and teamwork in nature.

SOCIAL COMMUNITY SERVICE

Taking part in swachhta (cleanliness) rallies, blood donation camps, tree plantations, and emergency traffic management.
